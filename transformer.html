Instrument Transformers

Instrument transformers are essential for accurate measurement and protection in electrical power systems. They step down high voltages and currents to manageable levels, enabling precise monitoring and control. Types include current transformers (CTs) and voltage transformers (VTs), each designed for specific functions. Instrument transformers ensure safety, enhance system reliability, and are crucial for the efficient operation of substations and other high-voltage installations.
